Channel Surfing

● Student Count: 8-15 students
● Maximum 5 mins
● List of 15 channels will be given on 14/09/2016
● No time will be given for preparation but properties can be used
● Unique features of channels are preferred

dance

● Student Count: 20-25 students
● Total time – 10 mins
● 8 mins (minimum 3 styles included) + 2min preparation
● External choreographers are not entertained
● Songs of selection will be screened on 23.09.2016 at 4pm
● Last minute changes are not allowed.

Theme Show

● Student Count: 15-18
● Time on stage – 10 mins (performance) + 2mins(for preparation)
● Theme is fixed by organizing and program committee
● Students could select theme based on lot on 15.09.2016
● Theme could be brought in different art forms except dance
● No of participants should be confirmed by 23.09.16

Spotlight

● Maximum 5-8 students.
● Time: 4mins
● It can be performed by individuals or as a team. Decorum of the college should be preserved. Mimic / Imitation on faculty is not allowed.

Music

● Student Count:14 -18, Maximum Time: 10mins + 2 mins(for preparation)
● Instruments should be brought by the students
● Fusion is not allowed
● Own composition is not allowed.
● Lyrics should be submitted on 10.1.2020

Short film

● Student Count: 20-25 per department including audio technicians (One Team)
● Maximum Duration : 4 min
● Works will be considered for competition only if they are of a total running time of 4 minutes or less, and were completed before 25.09.2016.
● All qualified entries must be independently produced and financed.
● Theme is fixed by organizing and program committee

Rangoli

● Color powder and Eco friendly materials can be used but not the salt to articulate the patterns
● 2 teams per Department
● 6 members in a team
● Allotted time is 1:30 hours (7:30 am-9:00 am )
● Space will be allotted by lot

Poetry Writing (English)

● Photo / Image will be given on the spot and a poem to be written based on the given image
● Student Count: 5-7 from each Department
● Time limit : 1 hour
● Participants will be judged based on their ability to bring out the theme &their language command

Poetry Writing (Tamil)

● Photo / Image will be given on the spot
● Student Count: 5-7 from each Department
● Time limit : 1 hour
● Participants will be judged based on their ability to bring out the theme &their language and command

Public Speaking – English

● 4 participants/Department
● Each participant will be given 4mins
● Vulgarity will lead to elimination; Jury decision is final and binding

Public Speaking – Tamil

● No. of Participants from each Department: 5
● Maximum time on dais: 3 –4mins
● Theme will be given 1 hour prior to the event (lot based selection)

Block and tackle

● 4 participants/Department
● Each participant will be given 4mins
● Vulgarity will lead to elimination; Jury decision is final and binding

Face Painting

● 4 teams each Department
● 2 participants per team
● Fancy your own imagination
● Face paints and Cosmetic Face glitters are allowed.
● Use only Brush &Sponge. No attachments or stencils allowed.
● Models may not paint themselves.

JAM

● Four participants from each Department
● 3 rounds (including Gibberish, will be decided by the programme in-charge )
● Participant should speak on the topic without Repetition, hesitation &deviation from the topic

Minute to Glory

● 5 participants in each Department
● Team / Individual event
● Martial Arts is not permitted

Pot Pourri

● TWO team per Department,
● FIVE participants per team
● One person enacts and the team members must guess. Every person in the team should take
a turn to enact.
● No sounds, lettering or splits per word are allowed
● Pointing to object or person or showing something are not allowed
● Pictionary round: The participant must draw the given topic and convey the meaning to
his/her team members

Ship Wreck

● Student Count: 4
● Time for defending : 4 min; Questions Session : 3 min
● Personalities will be allotted by lots

ADZAP

● ONE team of 10 participants from each Department
● Teams will pick lots with product names 15 mins prior to their performance
● Maximum time on stage will be 10 mins (plus 3 min question session)
● The judges can question the team on their product &presentation
● Vulgarity will lead to elimination

Connections

● Student Count: 3 per Department
● Two teams per Department

Essay Writing – English

● On spot title
● Time limit : 1 hour
● 4 members from each Department

Essay Writing – Tamil

● On spot title
● Time limit : 1 hour
● 4 members from each Department

Voice over

  • 2 teams
  • 2 members/Department
  • 3 min video will be played
  • Performance time: 3min
  • No time will be given for preparation

Photography

● Inside the college (Loyola campus)
● Theme will be given on spot
● Student Count: 5 from each Department
● Duration: 1 hour
● Content plays the role
● Date and time should be fixed in the camera

Poster design

● Theme will be given on the spot
● 6 entries per Department
● Maximum Time: 1 hour
● Participants will be required to design their posters on ‘Adobe Photoshop’
● Participants will be judged based on creativity &expertise
● Students will be given ONE hour for making the poster
● Pictures will be provided by the event in-charge

Master of Ceremony

● 4 individual participants per Department
● Time on stage will be 3 mins
● Participants can perform based on the situation given by the Judges

Spin A Yarn (Cook up a story) – English

● 4 entries per Department (individual)
● Images will be projected for which participants should develop a story
● Time: 3 min for presenting the story 2 min for question session

Spin A Yarn (Cook up a story) – Tamil

● 4 entries per Department (individual)
● Images will be projected for which participants should develop a story
● Time: 3 min for presenting the story 2 min for question session

Pencil Sketch

● The drawings must illustrate the competition theme mentioned on the spot.
● The drawing can be in pencil, pen or paint, in colour or black and white.
● The drawings must be in A4 or A3 format.
● Student Count: 8 from each Department
● Duration: 1 hour

Paper Crafts

  • 2 Team per Department.
  • 2 students per team
  • Performance time: 30 min
  • The materials required must be brought by the participant .
  • Color, type of sheets will be decided by students itself.
